About Us

Hello, my name is Frank Xu. I am a college student of New Jersey Institute of Technology. Other than creating this website for my semester grade, I also hope that it will bring more awareness to the amount of pollutants in our water and how it will affect us. The website itself is not official and the donations and volunteering form is not real. However, the main focus is to illustrate the need to focus our attention towards the ocean and how we can help heal the largest ecosystem on earth.
