What are pollutants

Before we talk about how to clean up the ocean, we need to first know what we are actually cleaning up. There are many things floating around in our oceans. Some are from animals while others are man made. The problem is that our oceans are being filled with man made trash that directly and indirectly harm the ecosystem. Some pollutants that will be discussed are: plastics, fertilizer overflow and oil spillage.

Plastics

Plastic has played an important part in making our lives easier. From plastic bottles to tires, whether you want to admit it or not, plastic is all around us. The great thing about plastic is their ability to last a long time. If you through a plastic bottle out in the streets, that bottle won't break down for a long time. This durability is also a problem that is causing major environmental damage. Because plastic products can come in different shapes and sizes, it's not uncommon to see animals have their stomach filled with small plastic trash or get strangled or entangled by plastic bags or fish nets.
